# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Security practitioners, platform engineers, and developers evaluating runtime protection for Kubernetes and cloud native workloads. They typically discover this site during research, community events, or while exploring KubeArmor docs.

## Product Purpose
KubeArmor.io communicates what KubeArmor does, why it matters for modern runtime security, and how to adopt it through docs, community channels, and focused campaign pages like the AI CTF. Success means visitors quickly understand the value, trust the technical depth, and move to participate, learn, or deploy.

## Brand Personality
Technical, credible, welcoming. The voice should feel practitioner-first, specific, and confident without hype.

## Anti-references
- Neon cyberpunk overload and visual noise that harms readability.
- Generic SaaS templates with interchangeable hero sections.
- Heavy marketing copy that hides concrete technical details.

## Design Principles
- Show operational clarity first: explain how things work with concrete steps and terms.
- Reward trust with specificity: include exact eligibility rules, budgets, and outcomes.
- Keep complexity approachable: security topics should feel challenging but navigable.
- Build momentum through structure: each section should naturally lead to the next action.
- Use visual personality with restraint: iconography and motion should support meaning, not decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Baseline accessibility support for keyboard users and readable contrast across key UI sections, with reduced-motion-safe interaction behavior where animation is used.
